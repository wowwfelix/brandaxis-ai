## What is BrandAxis?

BrandAxis is a SaaS platform for **AI brand monitoring and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** — think of it as analytics for the AI answer layer. It shows you not just *whether* your brand appears in AI answers, but *how* it appears, *why* it appears, and *what to do* to improve it.

Where SEO measures your visibility in Google's blue links, BrandAxis measures your visibility in the AI-generated answers that are increasingly replacing them.
